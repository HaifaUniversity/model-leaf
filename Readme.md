    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.xwl445igw1kq"><span class="c3">Name</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">model-leaf - the leaf model simple interface</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.lb2fkasp1tki"><span class="c3">Synopsis</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c9 c10 c13">model-leaf [--version] &nbsp;[--help] &lt;command&gt; [&lt;args&gt;]</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.n6edw955q866"><span>Description</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c10">Model-leaf uses the Tensorflow implementation of Mask-RCNN by MatterPort and a handful of integration </span><span>scripts </span><span class="c1">and utilities to simplify training and inference of leaf datasets.</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">For information on the different subcommands read the according manual pages</span></p>

    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.vs757bjjlsa2"><span class="c3">Name</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">model-leaf-train - train a model for leaves</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.s3usie8xs58c"><span class="c3">Synopsis</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c7 c9">model-leaf train [-o &lt;path&gt; | --output=&lt;path&gt;] [-k &lt;value&gt; | --dataset-keep=&lt;value&gt;] &nbsp;</span></p>
    <p class="c6 c22"><span class="c7 c9">[-t &lt;path&gt; | --test-set=&lt;path&gt;] [-c &lt;configfile&gt; | --config=&lt;configfile&gt;] </span></p>
    <p class="c6 c22"><span class="c18">[--preview-only] &lt;datasetpath&gt; &lt;bg-path&gt;</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.f4ydhq3vf44n"><span class="c3">Notes</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">&lt;datasetpath&gt; Should be the path to a json annotation file with valid relative paths to the images.</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">&lt;bg-path&gt; Path to a directory containing the synthetic backgrounds.</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.k91ce8kklme6"><span class="c3">Description</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span>Creates a dataset of synthetic pictures, and runs the training model on the dataset. The best result model is saved as </span><span>a .h5</span><span class="c1">&nbsp;file. </span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.w4aurxjju2q4"><span class="c3">Options</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.3jdy9anwq7f4"><span class="c8">-o &lt;path&gt;</span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.dp2dvuhwtzs2"><span class="c8">--output=&lt;path&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span>specify path </span><span>to .h5</span><span class="c1">&nbsp;model location [default: current]</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.4ifjz2ptsa3x"><span class="c8">-k &lt;value&gt;</span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.v55ap3ipb5kb"><span class="c8">&nbsp;--dataset-keep=&lt;value&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">specify how many samples to keep (default 0)</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.d0vyg61o11yr"><span class="c8">-t &lt;path&gt;</span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.xmwe6632668d"><span class="c8">--test-set=&lt;path&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">specify path to test set </span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.g8yk5ov69edf"><span class="c8">-c &lt;path&gt;</span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.xblny0jcvmsc"><span class="c8">--config=&lt;path&gt;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">specify path to the model (mask-r cnn) config file.</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.qa3j2itqqkk3"><span class="c8">-s (random | grouped)</span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.d235mo7ytyn"><span class="c8">--synthetic=(random | grouped)</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">Set the synthetic dataset generator to scatter the leaves randomly (cucumber), or to group the leaves around a base (banana)</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.e43zgchzc0yc"><span class="c8">--leaf-size-min=&lt;size&gt;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">Set the minimum size of leaves in the synthetic picture</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.sjnepm1lcw0b"><span class="c8">--leaf-size-max=&lt;size&gt;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">Set the maximum size of leaves in the synthetic picture</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.pv37v21e50gy"><span class="c8">--leaf-number-min=&lt;number&gt;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">Set the minimum number of leaves in the synthetic picture</span></p>
    <h6 class="c11 c12" id="h.u9q9slalsm1l"><span class="c8"></span></h6>
    <h6 class="c11" id="h.e4e6owcyrcz4"><span class="c8">--leaf-number-max=&lt;number&gt;</span></h6>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c1">Set the maximum number of leaves in the synthetic picture</span></p>
    <p class="c4"><span class="c1"></span></p>

    <p class="c0 subtitle" id="h.37ve31svu0d0"><span class="c3">Synopsis</span></p>
    <p class="c6"><span class="c7">model-leaf infer [</span><span class="c18">-o &lt;path&gt; | --output=&lt;path&gt;</span><span class="c7">] [--pictures-only</span><span class="c7 c9">&nbsp;| --contour-only] </span></p>
